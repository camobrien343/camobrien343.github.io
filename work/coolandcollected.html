Branding & Identity

Cool and Collected Antiques

Overview

Cool and Collected Antiques is known for their vintage and retro décor. The business specializes in unique items and one of a kind collections. Cool and Collected Antiques is modern and upbeat and this logo was created to represent their values as a friendly placed along with an open atmosphere.


The redesigned reflects Cool and Collected Antiques image as a vintage mall with a modern mood. In order to capture the artistic and eccentric atmosphere the logo design is friendly, inviting and stylish. Keeping with energetic colors, the tone shows the passion of the business and draws inspiration from vintage sign type and retro designs at the same time balancing with the logo feeling current and exciting.
