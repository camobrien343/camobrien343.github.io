KZUM 89.3 Giving Tuesday Shirt

Overview

For Giving Tuesday, KZUM created a shirt as a than-you for listeners and patrons who invest and donate to the station. The design pays tribute to the history of KZUM by incorporating a retro radio and vibrant colors. In celebration of Giving Tuesday, listeners who donated to support the station received the long-sleeve shirt as a gift.
