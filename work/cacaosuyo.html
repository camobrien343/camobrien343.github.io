Package Design

student work

Cacaosuyo

Overview

The tone of this packaging uses earthly and natural colors to capture the essence and nature of Peru’s vast geography. The design takes inspiration from Peru’s many floral species and rain-forest climate to create a modern design that fits today’s culture of well-designed items. The tone was crafted to show elegance and sustainability with an emphasis on typefaces that utilized a modern sans-serif. The packaging shows Cacaosuyo as down-to-earth, sensible, and practical while utilizing stylish, airy, and contemporary colors, typefaces, and patterns that bring Peru to people around the world.
