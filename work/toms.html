Toms Shoes Annual Report

Overview

Toms is a company known for helping others. The annual report was created to be vibrant, energetic, and earthy in its design and type. This project utilized features that included handmade elements combined with minimal design aesthetics.


Although the primary objective was to educate the audience about the performance of Toms, it was also crafted to be engaging, refreshing and inspiring not just with Toms information but also with its creative approach to how readers perceive annual reports.
