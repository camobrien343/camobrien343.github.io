Web Design & Development

Marz

Overview

The web design and development showcases the unique mood of Marz Bar located in Lincoln, Nebraska, by combining a clean and contemporary look while being easy to navigate and stylish. People who visit the site are engaged by the vibrant design that reflects Marz and it’s community. Visitors will get an understanding that Marz doesn’t take itself too seriously and appeals to a large community of free-thinkers.


Astrology patterns accent the page layout and help to create a cohesive and inviting appearance that viewers will want to continue exploring, not just for the business information but also it’s design aesthetic and quality that reflects the client.
