Moleskine Promotional Booklet

Overview

Moleskine prides themselves as a one of a kind tool for gathering ideas and the collective creative process. The promotional book design is playful, artistic, and inviting. The design shows their history as well as today’s creative mood of shared ideas and limitless possibilites. Artists want a sketchbook to always have with them and one that is easy to carry. The design also focuses on it’s ease of transportation and versitility of mediums that can be used on it.
